Discovering Temasek

Sang Nila Utama discovers the fishing village of Temasek and renames it as "Singapura".

Arrival of the British

Sir Stamford Raffles arrives in Singapore and establishes it as a British trading port.

Singapore Falls to the Japanese

The Japanese Occupation of Singapore during World War 2 begins after the British surrender.
